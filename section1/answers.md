# Section 1: Signal Acquisition

## a. Record three music files for 1 minutes each in different noisy environment (.mp3 files)

The three music files are provided in the `audio/mp3` folder.

## b. Load all three .wav files to MATLAB

The MATLAB script `signal_acquisition.m` loads the three .wav files using the `audioread()` function.

## c. Pot the time-domain signals of audio on one space

The `signal_acquisition.m` script plots the time-domain signals of the three audio files in a single figure with three subplots.

## d. Display Sampling frequency of each signal.

The sampling frequencies of the three signals are displayed in the MATLAB command window when the `signal_acquisition.m` script is executed.
